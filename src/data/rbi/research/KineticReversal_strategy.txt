STRATEGY_NAME: KineticReversal

STRATEGY_DETAILS:
The KineticReversal strategy is a classic mean-reversion system designed to capture short-term price corrections in high-liquidity environments like BTC-USD. By utilizing the Relative Strength Index (RSI) on a 15-minute timeframe, the strategy identifies periods where price momentum has become overextended (stretched) in either direction, betting on a return to the local mean.

### 1. Key Strategy Components
*   **Asset Class:** Cryptocurrency (Specifically optimized for BTC-USD).
*   **Timeframe:** 15-Minute (M15). This provides a balance between noise reduction and high-frequency trading opportunities.
*   **Market Logic:** Mean Reversion. The strategy assumes that extreme RSI readings are unsustainable and that price will eventually "snap back" to its average.

### 2. Entry/Exit Rules
*   **Long Entry (Buy):** Enter a long position when the RSI (14) crosses below the **30** level. This indicates the asset is technically "oversold" on the 15-minute lookback period.
*   **Short Exit/Sell (Close):** Close the long position (or initiate a sell) when the RSI (14) crosses above the **70** level. This indicates the asset has reached an "overbought" state and the upward momentum is likely exhausted.
*   **Execution Note:** Entries and exits should ideally be confirmed at the close of the 15-minute candle to avoid "wick" traps where the RSI briefly touches a level but retreats before the interval ends.

### 3. Risk Management
*   **Stop Loss Placement:** Since RSI can stay oversold in a strong downtrend, it is recommended to place a hard stop loss 1.5% - 2% below the entry price or below the recent swing low.
*   **Position Sizing:** Given the volatility of Bitcoin, Moon Dev recommends using a consistent risk-per-trade (e.g., 1-2% of total account equity).
*   **Trend Filtering (Optional Enhancement):** To avoid "falling knives," this strategy performs best in sideways or ranging markets. In a strong trending market, consider only taking trades that align with the higher timeframe trend (e.g., 4H timeframe).

### 4. Required Indicators
*   **Relative Strength Index (RSI):**
    *   **Length:** 14 periods.
    *   **Lower Bound:** 30 (Oversold).
    *   **Upper Bound:** 70 (Overbought).
    *   **Source:** Close Price.
